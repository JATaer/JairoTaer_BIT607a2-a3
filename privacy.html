Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you fill out our booking or contact forms. This includes:

  • Owner's Name
  • Email Address
  • Dog's Name
  • Dog's Breed and Age
  • Any comments or messages you send us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the purpose of providing our grooming services. This includes:

  • Contacting you to confirm your booking.
  • Replying to your inquiries.
  • Maintaining a record of your appointments.

3. Data Security

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Your Consent

By using our website and submitting our forms, you consent to our privacy policy.
